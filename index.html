Our Story

Carrying the Gospel into the high places

Christ Himalaya Ministry exists to bring the good news of Jesus Christ to the villages, ridgelines, and river valleys of the Himalayan region — places often reached only on foot, and often overlooked.

We plant churches, train local believers to lead, and walk alongside communities with practical care — because the Gospel is never only spoken, it is also lived out in service to our neighbors.

What began as a small circle of prayer has grown into a network of churches, workers, and volunteers united by one calling: to see Christ known and followed across every mountain community.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ throughout the Himalayan region by planting churches, training leaders, and serving communities in His name — from the highest ridge to the smallest village.

What We Believe

The foundations we build on

Four convictions shape every church we plant and every hand we extend.

Faith

Grounded in Scripture as the living Word of God, trusted above all other authority.

Love

Serving every community with the same compassion Christ showed us, without condition.

Hope

Anchored in the promise of Christ's return, even amid hardship and high altitude.

Service

Meeting practical needs — health, education, relief — as an expression of the Gospel.

Our Work

Ministries across the region

Every ministry is carried out by local believers who know their communities best.

Church Planting

Establishing local congregations in villages that have never had a resident church.

Leadership Training

Equipping pastors and elders with sound teaching so churches can stand on their own.

Children & Youth

Discipling the next generation through Bible clubs, mentorship, and schooling support.

Literacy & Scripture

Translation, literacy classes, and Scripture distribution in local languages and dialects.

Community Relief

Practical aid — food, medical outreach, disaster response — delivered in Christ's name.

Prayer & Mobilization

Uniting believers around the region in sustained prayer for the unreached.

Dear brothers and sisters in Christ,

Greetings to you all in the precious name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.

It is a privilege to serve through Christ Himalaya Ministry. Our ministry exists to proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ, make faithful disciples, and lead people into a life-changing relationship with God. We believe that our mission does not end when someone accepts Christ. True discipleship is a lifelong journey of faith, spiritual growth, and service.

At Christ Himalaya Ministry, we are committed to walking alongside people every day. We build faith through biblical teaching, prayer, worship, fellowship, and practical care. We equip believers with the knowledge of God’s Word through Bible training so they become spiritually mature, strong in faith, and firmly rooted in Christ.

Our vision is to bring people out of darkness into the marvelous light of Christ. We desire to see lives transformed, families restored, communities impacted, and nations reached through the power of the Gospel.

Our primary mission is to prepare people for eternity by leading them to Jesus Christ and helping them remain faithful until the end. We are committed to raising disciples who obey God’s Word, worship Him wholeheartedly, and become disciple-makers themselves.

We also believe in developing godly leaders. Leadership is not about position or authority—it is about serving with humility, integrity, wisdom, and love. Through leadership training, mentoring, and biblical education, we seek to equip men and women who will faithfully lead churches, ministries, and communities according to God’s will.

Worship is at the heart of our ministry. We are committed to raising true worshippers who worship the Father in spirit and in truth. Worship is not limited to songs; it is a lifestyle of surrender, holiness, obedience, and love for God.

As we move forward, let us remain united in prayer, faithful to God’s Word, passionate in evangelism, and committed to serving others with compassion. Together, we can fulfill the Great Commission by making disciples of all nations and sharing the hope of Jesus Christ with the world.

May Christ Himalaya Ministry continue to be a light to those in darkness, a place of hope for the broken, a training ground for disciples, a home for worshippers, and a source of godly leadership for generations to come.

May God bless you all, strengthen your faith, and use each one of you for His glory.

Thank you, and may the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you always.

God bless you all.
